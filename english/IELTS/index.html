    雅思

    雅思口语

    雅思口语介绍

    Part 1 Interview

    时间:4~5分钟

    内容:3个话题，每个话题2~4个问题;话题与日常生活相关，包括个人基本信息、喜好、技能、人物、物品、抽象话题等

    目的:考查对日常性观点和信息、常见的生活 经历或情形进行交流的能力

    Part 2 Presentation

    时间:3~4分钟:1分钟准备(可以做笔记)+1-2分钟答题

    内容:1个话题卡+3-4个提示问题；5大类话题:人物、物品、事件与经历、地点、媒体

    目的:考查能否恰当地运用语言、连贯地组织自己的观点

    Part 3 Discussion

    时间:4~5分钟

    内容:针对与Part 2话题相关的内容一展开更加深入、抽象的讨论;6大类问题:个人观点、对比比较问题解决、优缺点、前景预测、解释原因 目的:考查表达并解释观点、分析并讨论以及对事件进行推测的语言表达能力

    评分标准

    词汇丰富度（Lexical resource）

    语法范围及准确度（Grammatical range and accuracy）

    流利度与连贯性（Fluency and coherence）

    发音（Pronunciation）

    话题：StudiesWork

    本科生：I'm a student. I study ... at ... University

    研究生：I'm a student. I'm doing a master's degree in ...

    doctorate PhD bachelor's degree

    Why did you choose to study that/to do that type of work?

    ·In fact, it was my parents' idea, but I don't mind...(doing) because...

    ·it has a promising future

    ·graduates are in great demand

    ·It's well-paid

    ·there are good benefits and job security

    What was the most difficult thing about your studies/work?

    ·I found it really hard to ...

    ·I used to struggle in ...

    ·For some reason, I just couldn't ... no matter how hard I tried

    ·... But I'm working on it. / It's getting better

    ·It would have to be ... (trying to... /dealing with...)

    What's your future study/work plan?

    ·I'm planning to ... study for my master's degree/ get a qualificatoin in ...

    ·I hope that in five years I will be ... working for a multi-national company

    ·I'd like to ... see myself in a management position

    ·My goal is to ... set up my own business
